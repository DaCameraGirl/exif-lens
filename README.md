# EXIF Lens – by DaCameraGirl 📸

> Beautiful, private, local-only EXIF metadata viewer for photographers.

Drag a photo in → see every camera setting, instantly. No uploads, no tracking, 100% in-browser.

**Live:** https://nextjs-boilerplate-eight-alpha-13.vercel.app/

## Features

- 🎯 **Drag & drop** – or paste from clipboard
- 📷 **Full EXIF readout** – camera body, lens, aperture, shutter, ISO, focal length, flash, white balance, metering, and more
- 🗺️ **GPS map** – see exactly where the photo was taken (OpenStreetMap)
- 🔒 **100% private** – all processing happens in your browser, photos never leave your device
- 🧹 **Strip EXIF** – one-click clean download, removes all metadata before posting online
- 📦 **Batch support** – drop multiple photos, flip through them like a filmstrip
- 📋 **Copy JSON** – grab the raw EXIF data
- 🎨 **Beautiful UI** – dark, photographer-focused, fast

Supported formats: JPEG, HEIC, PNG, TIFF, WebP, AVIF

- **Leaflet / react-leaflet** – maps

## Why another EXIF viewer?

Most online EXIF tools are ugly, upload your photos to a server, or look like they're from 2005. EXIF Lens is:

- ✨ Actually nice to look at
- 🔐 Fully local – your photos never touch a server
- 📸 Built by a photographer, for photographers
- ⚡ Fast – no upload wait times
- 🆓 Free and open source
